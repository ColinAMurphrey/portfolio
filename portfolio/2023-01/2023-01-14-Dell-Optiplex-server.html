Dell Optiplex Server

Recently, I was able to acquire a Dell Optiplex from work so I can use it for projects. My IT department began recycling hardware they didn't need anymore. Naturally, given that I do a lot of projects with homemade servers, I asked about a Dell Optiplex that they seem to have forgotten about. My boss agreed to letting me have it.
